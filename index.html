About


a little about me

Hello! my name is Christian Hernandez and I am a web developer with a passion for front-end development and design. I'm currently a third-year at the University of Riverside pursuing a degree in Computer Science. I aspire a career that will allow me to channel my creativity by crafting beautiful designs and engaging experiences.

When I am not on the computer, I enjoy listening to music running, hiking, and watching anime. I am an enthusiast runner that likes a challenge whenever I run. The competitive side of running is what helps me improve while challenging friends as well! Music is what keeps me going whenever I reach my limit, it's an extension of my runners life and a part of my everyday life as well. Finally, anime has been a close part of my life since I can remember. Starting from watching Cowboy Bebop, Samurai Champloo and FullMetal Alchemist to watching Studio Ghibli movies like Princes Mononoke, Spirited Away, and Howl's Moving Castle. These passions are what defines me as a person and as a web developer, it is what I try to incorporate in any design or project that I do, and it is what helps me express myself in an atricts way. :)
